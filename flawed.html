Flawed Magic

Interactive Storytelling. A fairy tale in Witch world.

Personal Work in a Shared Storyworld

11/2016

Introduction

Flawed Magic is an interactive storytelling where you control every action of the character (Petra) and shape your own unique story . The story begin by an adoptee Petra who is passionate for Witchcraft but constantly barred from his Witch mother for some past reasons. As he falls in love with a Princess Hedda, he has to use his flawed magic to solve a string of problems caused by intertwined relationship between their families.

Dramatic Agency

I give the player dramatic agency through offering dramatic way of decision making. As the whole story is set in a witch world, I incorporate a lot of witch stuff when the player make choices.

For example, when the story goes to the situation that you sneak into the witch's workshop, I display several wichery ingredients for you to brew a magic portion. The princess's first impression of you will depend on what you make here.

Unfairness

One rule in my story is unfairness as your flawed magic always leads to unexpected outcomes.

For instance, you perform magic to turn yourself into a bird and carry Hedda down from the tower. But your magic expires, and you two fall down. You hurt yourself, but you catch Hedda, and she kisses you. So your flawed magic is actually rewarded. Reversely, when you make a bonfire to please Hedda with your magic, you end up burning her dress and making her angry.

Multisequential Narrative

I adopted an unsequential storytelling to enrich my storyworld. The player will uncover some past stories which affect the later decision making.

In day 3, for instance, you will uncover your mom's past relationship in a secret room through the love letter and the missing magic stone.

In this way, my story reflects the whole storyworld by sharing the history of the Mad King.

Story Endings

The ending of the story is based on your score which is determined by each of your choice. The best ending is that the Mad King's wicked magic is broken. His curse is broken as well. You come back to life through the bracelet magic which is activated by Hedda's teardrops. Your witch mom admits your relationship with Hedda. You and Hedda live happily ever after.

MadKing Storyworld

My story Flawed Magic is the second story in MadKing's storyworld sequence. In the Storyworld, the fairy tale kingdom of Lordaeron is ruled by a paranoid and irritable magical king--The Mad King Ludwig.

There are four stories in MadKing's storyworld. The stories all take place in the kingdom of Lordaeron. Lordaeron is an ancient kingdom with a medieval fairy tale aesthetic.

Below is the brief introduction of each story. (I am the illustrator for all four stories)

: An architect named Ms. Hoffman is stolen away and brought to the Mad King’s castle to help him renovate his castle. She soon realizes that she’s become ensnared in an insidious plot to overthrow the King.

: An adoptee Petra is passionate for Witchcraft but constantly barred from his Witch mother for some past reasons. As he falls in love with a Princess Hedda, he has to use his flawed magic to solve a string of problems caused by intertwined relationship between their families.

: During the festival celebrating the completion of the King’s castle, a man by the name of Gherman Jakobson is blamed for murder. Using his wits and deductive reasoning, Gherman must find evidence to both clear his name and find the real killer.

: A plague has hit the poor district of the kingdom. Charismatic thief Leopold has decided to save the people, especially his sick mother, by stealing a magical healing artifact from the king’s castle. He must navigate the castle and steal the artifact in time to save his mother and the low class citizens of Lordaeron.
