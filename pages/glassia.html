Glassia Application

Prototyping on glass design and glass blowing simulation.

Personal Work

11/2016

Introduction

This project is dedicated to the study of intervening into glass blowing learning process through the inclusion of an application. I began by directly engaged with a glass blowing class in Hotlanta Glass Blowing School in Atlanta. Through participatory observation and glass blowing practice, I went back with my understanding of glass blowing and then I prototyped an application to help people approach to this practice more easily with the aid of digital devices.
