Snow Squeeze

Pico-8 mini game design & development

Personal Work

05/2017

Introduction

Snow Squeeze is mini game developed on PICO-8 console. The player controls a little Jelly to search the food which can help maintain the energy used to jump forward. The goal of this game is to survive in the storm while collect as much star as possible.
