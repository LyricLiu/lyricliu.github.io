Halloween Haunters

Geolocation Based AR Mobile Game of Halloween Ghost Collection.

Personal Work

11/2016

Introduction

Halloween Haunters is a Location based AR game in which users hunt characters in real-world locations using smartphones. In the game, Witches, Skeletons, Ghosts, Vampires and Zombies â€“ all these horror supernatural beings are haunting around Georgia Tech campus, and your job is to find and capture them.

This game was launched just before 2016 Halloween to provide a unique Halloween experience for students in Georgia Tech.

Game Characters

I chose blocky pixel art for my 3D characters, for I want players to feel visually in a digital world when playing this game instead of being disappointed by fake reality characters. As Jason Rohrer's idea that pixel art is a kind of digitally native cartooning, my AR game tries to intervene into reality through digital characters and create a more appealing digital gaming experience in real life.

Game Map

The game map is converted from real world Georgia Tech map. I distributed characters around the map and visualized their locations by shining yellow dots. The color of the dot will turn green after the corresponding character is captured. And new characters unlocked will be displayed by position icons.
