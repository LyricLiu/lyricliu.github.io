U-stay App

Application prototyping. Creating more meaningful talks for couples.

Role: Visual Design, UI / UX

12/2014

Introduction

When you love someone, you want to be as connected to them as possible, espeacially in times when you are set miles apart for some reason. U-stay is a beautifully designed social network for couples to better connect with each other.

Make Your Own Character

Customize your virtual character that would be displayed on your lover's device.

You are allowed to set your mood, your status and schedules to share.

Certainly, you are able to view the character customized by your lover.

Flowchart

Three main modules:

: View virtual character of your lover including his/her weather, mood, schedule, etc.

: Customize your own character, change your mood, update your schedule, etc.

: Upload files for share. (Video, Text Message, Picture)
